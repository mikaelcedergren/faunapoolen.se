<h2>From sterile pool to living water</h2><p>A pool conversion can replace a tired chlorine pool with a more ecological swimming pond. The design needs to account for filtration, circulation, edges and the existing structure.</p><h2>What changes</h2><p>The water system, visual character and maintenance rhythm all change. The goal is to create a stable ecosystem rather than a chemically controlled basin.</p>
