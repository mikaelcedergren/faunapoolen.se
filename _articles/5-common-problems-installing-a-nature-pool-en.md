<h2>Unbalanced ecosystem</h2><p>If the biological system is not balanced, algae and cloudy water can become a problem.</p><h2>Poor placement</h2><p>Too much sun or too much shade can affect water quality and plant health.</p><h2>Leaks and weak sealing</h2><p>A natural pool needs careful ground preparation and reliable liner installation.</p><h2>Insufficient circulation</h2><p>Moving water supports oxygenation and filtration. Still water is harder to keep healthy.</p><h2>Wrong plants</h2><p>The plants must suit the climate, water depth and filtration role.</p>
