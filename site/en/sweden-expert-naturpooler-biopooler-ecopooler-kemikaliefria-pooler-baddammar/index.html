Why Faunapoolen was founded

Faunapoolen was born out of a passion for uniting sustainability, beauty, and functionality in outdoor environments. As Sweden's leading expert on natural pools, biopools, ecopools, and chemical-free pools, our mission is to design and create water solutions that blend into nature and promote an ecological lifestyle.

Our story has been shaped by industry leaders who have redefined what is possible in water feature design. From the innovative work of Team Aquascape to the craftsmanship of Tussey Landscaping, Atlantis Water Gardens, California Waterscapes, and Pondscapes AZ, we have drawn inspiration, insights, and techniques from the very best. Their commitment to quality, creativity, and sustainability aligns perfectly with our values.

Faunapoolen was founded to meet the growing demand for chemical-free pools and swimming ponds that promote health, biodiversity, and relaxation. By combining inspiration from global pioneers with our own expertise, we have become a reliable and knowledgeable partner for unique chemical-free pools and outdoor environments across Sweden.

Whether you dream of a peaceful natural pool, a modern ecological pool, or a picturesque swimming pond, Faunapoolen is here to realize your vision through designs that elevate both aesthetics and sustainability.
