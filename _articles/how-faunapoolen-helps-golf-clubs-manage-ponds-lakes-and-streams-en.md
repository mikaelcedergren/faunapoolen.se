<h2>Water as an asset and a risk</h2><p>Well-managed water features can frame a course, support irrigation and improve the player experience. Poorly managed water can lead to algae, flooding, erosion and reputation damage.</p><h2>Ecological management</h2><p>Faunapoolen works with nature-based methods, filtration, maintenance planning and design improvements to make course water more resilient.</p>
