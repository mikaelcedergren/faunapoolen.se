<h2>A healthier swimming experience</h2><p>Natural pools avoid chlorine and rely on biological filtration, making the water feel softer and more connected to the garden.</p><h2>Designed for the landscape</h2><p>Instead of looking like a separate object, a natural pool can be shaped with stone, planting and edges that belong in the outdoor environment.</p><h2>Long-term enjoyment</h2><p>With the right design, a natural pool becomes both a place to swim and a living water feature that changes beautifully through the seasons.</p>
