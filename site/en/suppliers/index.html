Our suppliers

To create sustainable water landscapes and inspiring outdoor environments, we collaborate with some of the most respected players in the industry. Our partners share our focus on quality, sustainability, and design, providing us access to products and solutions that meet the highest standards.

Aquascape is a world leader in the design and technology of water landscapes, with systems that offer both long-term functionality and easy maintenance. R. Moerings B.V. is Europe's most recognized grower of aquatic plants, supplying species that enhance biodiversity, improve water quality, and bring life to every project.

Dangarden represents Scandinavian design and reliable solutions for outdoor living, while Stenbolaget, Sweden's largest supplier of natural stone, offers materials for everything from paving to pool environments. For plumbing solutions, we collaborate with Micke Nilsson, a local player known for high service and professionalism.

Together, these partners give us the opportunity to offer solutions that are aesthetic, sustainable, and built to last over time.
