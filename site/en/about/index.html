About Faunapoolen: Swedish specialist in ecological pools

At Faunapoolen, we are passionate about creating beautiful and sustainable water landscapes that blend into their surroundings. We believe in combining smart design with nature's own purification processes to create a bathing experience free from chemicals – a place where both people and nature thrive.

Focusing on quality, ecology, and aesthetics, we help you realize your dream of a natural pool and other natural water landscapes. Whether you want a swim-friendly pond or a beautiful water landscape, we guide you through the entire process – from planning to finished pool.
